Why This Problem Demands a Hardware Solution
📱

Phone-Based Tools Create Friction

Current AI documentation tools require a phone on the desk or in hand. In sterile fields, during procedures, or in high-acuity situations, this is impractical or impossible.

⚖️

Provider-Patient Disputes Are Rising

Verbal consent disputes, allegations of misconduct, and documentation gaps are increasing. No current device provides an on-body record that protects both parties in real time.

🏥

EHR Systems Aren't Designed for Humans

EHRs were built for billing, not for clinicians. The documentation burden they create is a primary driver of the physician shortage — 1 in 5 physicians plans to leave medicine within 2 years.
